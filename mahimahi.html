Mahi-Mahi

Ingredients

  • mahi-mahi
  • white rice
  • asperagus
  • garlic bread
  • seasonings: salt, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and lemon pepper

Cooking Directions

  1. Peel potatoes. Then cut them into cubes.
  2. Toss Potatoes in bowl and season them.
  3. After seasonings, toss cubed potatoes into glass dish, cover with foil, and put into oven at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
  4. Once potatoes are tender, take them out, and put whole baby tomatoes as well as fresh mozerella on top. Then stick them back into the oven for about 10 minutes.
  5. Once tomatoes are tender, take out, let it sit and serve!
